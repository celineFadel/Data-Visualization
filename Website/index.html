How about refugees in Lebanon?
The refugees of Lebanon
Even with the instability in Lebanon, many people fled war and famine in their counrty and seeked refuge in Lebanon. In fact, Lebanon welcomed so many refugees that the UNRWA declared it the country hosting the largest number of refugees per capita.
